Caramel Mochi

A chewy mochi shell filled with velvety cream cheese and a molten caramel core. Coated in a glossy caramel glaze and finished with a crunch of sugar crystals for the perfect sweet-and-savory bite. The pillowy exterior is soft and bouncy to bite and it opens up to a rich and deeply sweet blend of burnt sugar.
