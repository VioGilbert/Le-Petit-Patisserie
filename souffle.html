Japanese Souffle Pancake

Fluffy Japanese souffle pancakes with caramel and a scoop of vanilla ice cream. These pancakes are tall, cottony stacks that taste like eating a cloud. The ice cream and caramel creates a blend of sweetness that makes this souffle pancake a bestseller in our cafe. Our chef takes extra care in creating these famous pancakes so that our customers can taste the delicate, cloud-like texture of these cottony confections.
