Blueberry Cheesecake

A sweet and sour taste of blueberry and a mix of our own cheesecake recipe. It offers the ultimate sweet-and-tart balance, matching rich cheese cream with bright, jammy fruit. It is a timeless bestseller of ours that keeps the palate refreshed with every bite. Perfect for people who find biscoff cheesecakes too heavy or cloying, the inclusion of fresh fruit makes blueberry cheesecake feel much lighter and cleaner.

  • Eggs
  • Wheat
  • Soy
  • Fruit allergy (Berries)
