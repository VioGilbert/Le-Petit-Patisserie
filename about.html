ABOUT US

Vision: To fuel the city's daily rhythm with artisanal grab-and-go good bakes and exceptional coffee.

Mission: To make every customer smile with our warm service and irresistible treats, to listen, learn, and evolve with our customers ensuring every visit to Le Petit Patisserie leaves them happier than before.

Our History

2024

We began our journey from a fancy shop but from a small kitchen. At that time, we only had four menu items which was the Le Petit Crossaint, Le Petit Loaf, Strawbery Sandwich, and Le Petit Egg Tart. Each item was carefully chosen to represent our identity as a patisserie that values simplicity and quality.

2025

The year 2025 marked the biggest turning point for Le Petit Patisserie. The overwhelming response from our customers gave us the confidence to go big. We finally opened our very first physical bakery shop that also provide dine-in services. Customers could now enjoy not only our four original items but also a wide variety of pastries, breads, cakes, and beverages.

2026

Now, Le Petit Patisserie have stepped into the digital era with the launch of our official website and online menu system, making it easier for customers to order their favourite pastries and drinks without the need to queue. This approach is sustainable, cost-saving, and efficient. Our menu continues to grow with each passing year, to spread the joy of Le Petit Patisserie to more neighbourhoods and pastries enthuasiasts.
